Travel & Hunt

Rumor says Mystic Glyphs are clues that lead to the late Riddle Master's treasures. Amateur glyph hunting is a good activity for adventurous travelers.

Mission & Glyphs

Each mission consists of 6 glyphs, they are mini challenges that take you through tourist attractions.

Hunters' Rules

Lastly, follow these rules so other hunters can also enjoy the experience:


  • 1) Be stealthy! Don't alarm the guards and citizens.

  • 2) Return physical glyphs to where you found them.

  • 3) Don't spoil the glyph secrets.
