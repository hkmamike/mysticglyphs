Mystic Glyphs

Buddy, welcome to the hunt.

Travel & Hunt

The late Riddle Master hid his treasures all over the world and mystic glyphs are the clues that lead to them. Typically, glyphs are located near landmarks and tourism areas.

While serious hunters strive to become the Master's successor, amateur glyphs hunting is a good activity for travelers who want to add some adventurous elements to their trip.

Missions & Campaigns

There are glyphs all over the world and our search team is working hard to identify them. To begin, select a city with glyphs identified.

Every mission consists of 6 glyphs, completing a mission leads to the next. Each campaign, typically made up of 3-4 missions, covers popular landmarks in an area and takes 3-4 hours to complete.

Glyphs

Each glyph is a mini challenge. Solving a glyph progresses you toward mission completion and provides you with additional information for the other glyphs in the mission.

When at least 4 glyphs are solved, activation clue for the next mission becomes available. Some glyphs are super challenging so you don't have to solve all of them, 4 is enough!

Hunters' Rules

Lastly, follow these rules so other hunters can also enjoy the experience:

  • 1) Be stealthy! Don't alarm the guards and citizens.
  • 2) Return glyphs to where you found them, they are the Riddle Master's legacy.
  • 3) Don't spoil the glyph secrets.
